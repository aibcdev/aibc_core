## Key Takeaways

### 1. Blaze.ai's Best Coverage = High Cost
- Blaze.ai dominates high-volume keywords like "AI content generator"
- These keywords have high CPC ($2.50-4.00) and high competition
- **AIBC Strategy:** Compete selectively, focus on unique value props

### 2. Blaze.ai's Weak Spots = AIBC Opportunities
- **Zero coverage** on AIBC's unique features (Brand DNA, Digital footprint scanner)
- **Weak coverage** on multi-modal content (Blaze.ai only does text)
- **No video/audio** content generation
- **AIBC Strategy:** Dominate these keywords with low CPC, high conversion

### 3. Lowest CPC, Highest ROI Keywords
- **Digital footprint scanner:** $0.50 CPC, zero competition
- **Brand DNA extraction:** $0.80 CPC, zero competition
- **Autonomous content agent:** $1.20 CPC, low competition
- **AIBC Strategy:** Invest heavily in these - they're AIBC's moat

### 4. Competitive Keywords Worth Pursuing
- **Brand voice AI:** $1.80 CPC, medium competition, AIBC advantage
- **AI content platform for startups:** $1.50 CPC, low competition, AIBC fit
- **Affordable AI content:** $1.60 CPC, low competition, AIBC pricing advantage
- **AIBC Strategy:** Win with better features and pricing

### 5. High-Volume Keywords (Selective)
- **AI content generator:** High traffic, high cost, can compete with unique features
- **AI writing tool:** High traffic, high cost, brand awareness play
- **AIBC Strategy:** Compete for brand awareness, but don't rely on these for conversions
