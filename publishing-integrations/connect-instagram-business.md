# Connect AIBC to Instagram Business

Connect your Instagram Business account to publish content directly from AIBC.

## Prerequisites

**Before connecting:**
- You need an **Instagram Business account** (not personal account)
- Your Instagram account must be linked to a **Facebook Page**
- You need admin access to the Facebook Page
- Instagram account must be active and in good standing

**How to convert to Business account:**
1. Open Instagram app
2. Go to Settings → Account
3. Switch to Professional Account
4. Choose Business
5. Link to Facebook Page

## How to Connect

### Step 1: Navigate to Integrations

1. Go to **Integrations** in left sidebar (or `/integrations`)
2. Find **Instagram** in the integrations list
3. Click **"Connect"** button

### Step 2: Enter Instagram Handle

1. **Enter your Instagram handle:**
   - Format: `@username` or just `username`
   - Example: `@yourbrand` or `yourbrand`
2. Click **"Verify"** or **"Continue"**

### Step 3: Verify Account

**Account verification:**
- AIBC verifies the account exists
- Checks if it's a Business account
- Confirms account is accessible
- Shows account details for confirmation

**If verification fails:**
- Check handle is correct
- Ensure it's a Business account
- Verify account is public (if required)
- Contact support if issues persist

### Step 4: Facebook Authorization

**Since Instagram Business requires Facebook:**
1. You'll be redirected to Facebook
2. **Log in to Facebook** (if not already)
3. **Authorize AIBC** to access:
   - Your Facebook Pages
   - Instagram account linked to pages
   - Publishing permissions
4. **Select the Facebook Page** linked to your Instagram
5. **Grant permissions:**
   - Read Instagram account info
   - Publish to Instagram
   - Manage Instagram content
6. Click **"Authorize"** or **"Allow"**

### Step 5: Complete Connection

**After authorization:**
- Connection is established
- Account is verified
- Sync status shows "Connected"
- You can now publish to Instagram

## Connection Status

### Connected

**What you'll see:**
- ✅ Green checkmark or "Connected" status
- Instagram handle displayed
- Account name and avatar
- Last sync time
- Sync status: "Synced" or "Syncing"

**You can now:**
- Publish posts to Instagram
- Schedule Instagram content
- View Instagram in Content Hub filters
- Use Instagram-specific features

### Syncing

**During sync:**
- Status shows "Syncing..."
- Account info is being retrieved
- May take a few moments
- Wait for sync to complete

### Error Status

**If connection fails:**
- Status shows "Error" or "Failed"
- Error message displayed
- Try reconnecting
- Contact support if persists

## What You Can Do After Connecting

### Publish to Instagram

**From Content Hub:**
1. Create or select content
2. Choose **Instagram** as platform
3. Select post type:
   - **Feed Post**: Regular image/video post
   - **Reel**: Short-form video
   - **Story**: Temporary story (24 hours)
   - **Carousel**: Multiple images
4. Add images/videos
5. Write caption
6. Add hashtags
7. **Publish** or **Schedule**

### Schedule Instagram Content

**Using Calendar:**
1. Go to **Calendar**
2. Create new event or select content
3. Choose **Instagram** platform
4. Set date and time
5. Content publishes automatically

### Cross-Post to Instagram

**From other platforms:**
- Create content for multiple platforms
- Include Instagram in platform selection
- Content adapts for Instagram format
- Publish to all selected platforms

## Instagram-Specific Features

### Post Types

**Feed Posts:**
- Single image or video
- Square (1:1) or portrait (4:5) aspect ratio
- Caption up to 2,200 characters
- Up to 30 hashtags
- Location tagging

**Reels:**
- Short-form video (15-90 seconds)
- Vertical (9:16) aspect ratio
- Music and effects
- Caption and hashtags

**Stories:**
- Temporary (24 hours)
- Vertical (9:16) aspect ratio
- Can add to Highlights
- Interactive elements

**Carousels:**
- Multiple images (up to 10)
- Swipeable gallery
- Caption applies to all
- Each image can have different aspect ratio

### Image Requirements

**Feed Posts:**
- **Aspect ratio**: 1:1 (square) or 4:5 (portrait)
- **Size**: 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1350px (portrait)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Max file size**: 8MB

**Stories:**
- **Aspect ratio**: 9:16 (vertical)
- **Size**: 1080x1920px
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Max file size**: 8MB

**Reels:**
- **Aspect ratio**: 9:16 (vertical)
- **Size**: 1080x1920px
- **Format**: MP4
- **Max file size**: 100MB
- **Duration**: 15-90 seconds

## Managing Your Connection

### View Connection Details

**In Integrations:**
- See connected account
- View account name and handle
- Check sync status
- See last sync time

### Re-sync Account

**If data seems outdated:**
1. Go to **Integrations**
2. Find Instagram connection
3. Click **"Sync"** or **"Refresh"**
4. Wait for sync to complete

### Disconnect Instagram

**To disconnect:**
1. Go to **Integrations**
2. Find Instagram connection
3. Click **"Disconnect"** or **"Remove"**
4. Confirm disconnection

**What happens:**
- Connection is removed
- Cannot publish to Instagram
- Existing scheduled posts may be affected
- Can reconnect anytime

## Troubleshooting

### Connection Fails

**Common issues:**
- **Not a Business account**: Convert to Business account
- **Not linked to Facebook**: Link Instagram to Facebook Page
- **Wrong permissions**: Re-authorize with correct permissions
- **Account suspended**: Check Instagram account status

**Solutions:**
- Verify account type
- Check Facebook Page link
- Re-authorize connection
- Contact Instagram support if account issues

### Publishing Fails

**Common issues:**
- **Image format wrong**: Use JPG or PNG
- **File too large**: Compress images
- **Aspect ratio wrong**: Use correct dimensions
- **Caption too long**: Instagram has 2,200 character limit
- **Hashtags issue**: Max 30 hashtags

**Solutions:**
- Check image requirements
- Verify file size
- Use correct aspect ratio
- Review caption length
- Limit hashtags

### Sync Issues

**If sync fails:**
- Check internet connection
- Verify account is still active
- Re-sync manually
- Disconnect and reconnect
- Contact support if persists
