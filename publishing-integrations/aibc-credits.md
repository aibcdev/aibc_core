# AIBC Credits

AIBC uses a credit system for certain premium features. Understanding credits helps you manage usage and costs effectively.

## What Are Credits?

Credits are used for:
- **AI image generation**: Production Room image creation
- **AI audio generation**: Production Room audio creation  
- **AI video generation**: Production Room video creation
- **Content regeneration**: Regenerating existing content
- **Deep Scans**: Premium Brand Scan type (uses separate scan credits)

**Note:** Basic content generation, Standard Scans, and most features don't use credits.

## Credit Allowances by Tier

### Free Tier
- **Credits per month**: 0 (no Production Room access)
- **Content generation**: Included (no credits)
- **Standard Scans**: Included (no credits)

### Pro Tier
- **Credits per month**: 100 credits
- **Content generation**: Included (no credits)
- **Standard Scans**: Included (no credits)
- **Deep Scans**: Uses separate scan credits (not monthly credits)

### Enterprise Tier
- **Credits per month**: 500 credits
- **Content generation**: Included (no credits)
- **Standard Scans**: Included (no credits)
- **Deep Scans**: Uses separate scan credits (not monthly credits)

## Credit Costs

### Production Room Costs

**Image Generation:**
- **Short/Simple**: ~5 credits
- **Mid/Moderate**: ~10 credits
- **Long/Complex**: ~20 credits

**Audio Generation:**
- **Short**: ~15 credits
- **Mid**: ~30 credits
- **Long**: ~50 credits

**Video Generation:**
- **Short**: ~25 credits
- **Mid**: ~50 credits
- **Long**: ~100 credits

**Note:** Exact costs may vary. Check Production Room for current pricing before generating.

### Other Credit Uses

**Content Regeneration:**
- ~1-2 credits per regeneration
- Varies by content length and complexity

**Deep Scans:**
- Uses separate scan credits (not monthly credits)
- ~7.00 per scan (included in scan cost, not credit system)

## Checking Your Credit Balance

### Dashboard View

**Quick view:**
- Dashboard shows credit balance at top
- Visual indicator of usage
- Remaining credits displayed
- Usage percentage shown

### Detailed View

**Settings → Billing:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** section
2. Find **"Credits"** section
3. **See:**
   - Total monthly allowance
   - Credits used this month
   - Credits remaining
   - Usage breakdown by feature
   - Reset date (when credits refresh)

### Production Room View

**Before generating:**
- Production Room shows credit cost
- Your current balance
- Whether you have enough credits
- Warning if balance is low

## Managing Credits

### Using Credits Efficiently

**Best practices:**
- **Check costs before generating**: See credit cost first
- **Use simpler styles when possible**: Lower credit costs
- **Batch similar requests**: More efficient usage
- **Review before generating**: Avoid regenerating unnecessarily

### When Credits Run Low

**Warning indicators:**
- Dashboard shows low balance warning
- Production Room warns before generating
- Email notification (if enabled)

**Options:**
- **Wait for reset**: Credits refresh monthly
- **Purchase credit pack**: Buy additional credits
- **Upgrade plan**: Get more monthly credits
- **Use credits wisely**: Prioritize important requests

### Purchasing Additional Credits

**How to purchase:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Find **"Purchase Credits"** section
3. Select credit pack size
4. Review pricing
5. Complete payment
6. Credits added immediately

**Credit pack options:**
- Various sizes available
- Pricing varies by pack
- Check current pricing in Settings

## Credit Reset

### Monthly Reset

**When credits reset:**
- **Reset date**: Based on your billing cycle
- **Automatic**: Credits refresh automatically
- **No carryover**: Unused credits don't carry to next month (unless specified)
- **Notification**: Email when credits reset (if enabled)

**Check reset date:**
- Settings → Billing → Credits section
- Shows next reset date
- Countdown to reset

## Credit Limits and Restrictions

### What Happens When You Run Out

**Production Room:**
- Cannot generate new images/audio/video
- Can still use Media Library
- Can upload your own media
- Can purchase more credits

**Content Regeneration:**
- May be limited
- Basic regeneration may still work
- Advanced regeneration may require credits

**Other features:**
- Most features don't require credits
- Content generation continues
- Scans continue (Standard Scans)

### Tier Limits

**Free tier:**
- No credits
- No Production Room access
- Upgrade for credits

**Pro tier:**
- 100 credits/month
- Can purchase more
- Upgrade for more monthly credits

**Enterprise tier:**
- 500 credits/month
- Can purchase more
- Highest monthly allowance

## Common Questions

### Do Credits Expire?

**Monthly credits:**
- Reset monthly (don't carry over)
- Expire at end of billing cycle
- Purchased credits may have different terms

**Purchased credits:**
- Check terms when purchasing
- May have expiration dates
- Contact support for details

### Can I Get Refunds?

**Credit refunds:**
- Generally no refunds for used credits
- Unused purchased credits may be refundable
- Contact support for refund requests

### What If I Need More?

**Options:**
- **Purchase credit pack**: Buy additional credits
- **Upgrade plan**: Get more monthly credits
- **Wait for reset**: Monthly refresh
- **Contact support**: Discuss options
