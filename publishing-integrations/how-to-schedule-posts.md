# How to schedule posts in AIBC

Scheduling posts lets you plan your content in advance and publish automatically at the right time. AIBC offers flexible scheduling options for all your content.

## Why Schedule Posts

**Benefits:**
- **Plan ahead**: Create content in advance
- **Optimal timing**: Publish when your audience is active
- **Consistency**: Maintain regular posting schedule
- **Efficiency**: Batch create and schedule
- **Time zones**: Schedule for different time zones

## Scheduling Methods

### Method 1: Schedule from Content Hub

**When creating or editing content:**

1. Go to **Content Hub**
2. **Create new content** or **select existing content**
3. **After writing content:**
   - Click **"Schedule"** button (instead of "Publish Now")
   - Or find **"Schedule"** option in content actions
4. **Set schedule:**
   - **Date**: Select publish date
   - **Time**: Select publish time
   - **Time zone**: Choose time zone (defaults to your account timezone)
5. **Select platforms**: Choose which platforms to publish to
6. Click **"Schedule"** or **"Save Schedule"**

**Content status changes:**
- Moves to **"Scheduled"** status
- Appears in **Calendar** view
- Will publish automatically at scheduled time

### Method 2: Schedule from Calendar

**Using the Calendar view:**

1. Go to **Calendar** (`/calendar`)
2. **Navigate to desired date:**
   - Use month/week navigation
   - Click on specific date
3. **Create scheduled event:**
   - Click **"+"** or **"Add Event"**
   - Or drag content from Content Hub to calendar date
4. **Set schedule:**
   - Date is pre-selected (the date you clicked)
   - Set time
   - Choose platforms
5. **Select or create content:**
   - Choose existing content from library
   - Or create new content inline
6. Click **"Schedule"**

**Benefits:**
- Visual planning
- See all scheduled content at once
- Easy to reschedule by dragging
- Plan weeks/months ahead

### Method 3: Bulk Schedule

**Schedule multiple posts at once:**

1. Go to **Content Hub**
2. **Select multiple content items:**
   - Check boxes next to content
   - Or use "Select All" for filtered results
3. Click **"Bulk Actions"** → **"Schedule"**
4. **Set schedule options:**
   - **Same time for all**: All posts at same date/time
   - **Spread over time**: Distribute posts over date range
   - **Specific times**: Set individual times (advanced)
5. **Choose platforms** for each
6. Click **"Schedule Selected"**

**Use cases:**
- Schedule week's content at once
- Distribute posts evenly
- Batch scheduling for campaigns

## Scheduling Options

### Date and Time Selection

**Setting date:**
- **Calendar picker**: Click to select date
- **Quick options**: Today, Tomorrow, Next Week
- **Custom date**: Enter specific date
- **Date range**: For bulk scheduling

**Setting time:**
- **Time picker**: Select hour and minute
- **Quick times**: Common posting times
- **Best time suggestions**: Based on your analytics (if available)
- **Custom time**: Enter specific time

### Time Zone Settings

**Default time zone:**
- Uses your account timezone (Settings → Profile)
- Can override per post
- All times shown in your timezone

**Changing timezone:**
- Set in Settings → Profile → Timezone
- Or override when scheduling individual posts
- Calendar shows times in your timezone

### Platform Selection

**Choose platforms:**
- **Single platform**: Schedule to one platform
- **Multiple platforms**: Schedule to all selected
- **Platform-specific times**: Different times per platform (if supported)

**Platform considerations:**
- Each platform may have optimal posting times
- Can schedule same content to multiple platforms
- Content adapts to platform requirements

## Viewing Scheduled Posts

### Calendar View

**See all scheduled content:**
1. Go to **Calendar**
2. **View options:**
   - **Month view**: See entire month
   - **Week view**: Detailed weekly view
   - **Day view**: Hour-by-hour schedule
3. **Scheduled posts appear:**
   - On their scheduled date
   - Color-coded by platform or status
   - Show time and platform
   - Click to see details

### Content Hub View

**Filter by scheduled:**
1. Go to **Content Hub**
2. **Filter by status:**
   - Select **"Scheduled"** filter
   - See all scheduled content
   - View scheduled date/time
   - See platforms

### List View

**Scheduled content list:**
- Shows all scheduled posts
- Sorted by scheduled date/time
- Shows platform, status, content preview
- Quick actions available

## Managing Scheduled Posts

### Editing Scheduled Posts

**To edit:**
1. Find scheduled post in **Calendar** or **Content Hub**
2. Click to open details
3. **Edit options:**
   - **Edit content**: Change text, images, etc.
   - **Reschedule**: Change date/time
   - **Change platforms**: Add/remove platforms
   - **Cancel schedule**: Remove from schedule
4. **Save changes**

**What happens:**
- Content updates
- Schedule remains (unless changed)
- Will publish with updates

### Rescheduling Posts

**To reschedule:**
1. Find scheduled post
2. **Option 1 - Calendar:**
   - Drag to new date/time
   - Drop on desired slot
3. **Option 2 - Content Hub:**
   - Click post → **"Reschedule"**
   - Select new date/time
   - Click **"Update Schedule"**

**Rescheduling options:**
- **Move to different date**: Change date only
- **Change time**: Keep date, change time
- **Move to different day**: Full reschedule

### Unscheduling Posts

**To remove from schedule:**
1. Find scheduled post
2. Click **"Unschedule"** or **"Remove from Schedule"**
3. Confirm action
4. **Content status changes:**
   - Moves to "Draft" or "Approved"
   - No longer scheduled
   - Can be scheduled again later

### Deleting Scheduled Posts

**To delete:**
1. Find scheduled post
2. Click **"Delete"**
3. Confirm deletion
4. **Content is permanently deleted**

**Warning:** Deletion cannot be undone.

## Automatic Publishing

### How It Works

**When scheduled time arrives:**
- AIBC automatically publishes content
- Posts to selected platforms
- Updates status to "Published"
- Sends notification (if enabled)

**Requirements:**
- **Auto-publish enabled**: Settings → Content Preferences
- **Platforms connected**: Must have active connections
- **Content approved**: If approvals required, must be approved first
- **No errors**: Content must be valid for platform

### Enabling Auto-Publish

**To enable:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Content Preferences**
2. Find **"Automatic Publishing"** option
3. Toggle **"Enable Auto-Publish"** on
4. **Configure:**
   - Require approval before publishing (optional)
   - Retry failed posts (optional)
   - Notification preferences
5. Click **"Save"**

### Disabling Auto-Publish

**To disable:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Content Preferences**
2. Toggle **"Enable Auto-Publish"** off
3. **Scheduled posts will:**
   - Remain scheduled
   - Not publish automatically
   - Require manual publishing
   - Show notification when time arrives

## Scheduling Limits

### Tier-Based Limits

**Scheduled posts count toward:**
- **Weekly content limits**: Based on your tier
- **Free**: 10 pieces/week total (scheduled + published)
- **Pro**: 50 pieces/week total
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited

**Planning:**
- Count scheduled posts in your planning
- Leave room for immediate posts
- Monitor your usage

## Troubleshooting

### Posts Not Publishing

**Common issues:**
- **Auto-publish disabled**: Enable in Settings
- **Platform disconnected**: Reconnect platform
- **Content errors**: Fix content issues
- **Time zone mismatch**: Check timezone settings
- **Approval required**: Approve content first

**Solutions:**
- Check Settings → Content Preferences
- Verify platform connections
- Review content for errors
- Check timezone
- Approve pending content

### Scheduling Errors

**If scheduling fails:**
- Check content is valid
- Verify platforms are connected
- Ensure within tier limits
- Check date/time is in future
- Contact support if persists
