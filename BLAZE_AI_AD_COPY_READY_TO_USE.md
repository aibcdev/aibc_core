## Performance Tracking

### Key Metrics to Monitor
1. **CTR (Click-Through Rate)** - Target: 3-6%
2. **Conversion Rate** - Target: 3-5%
3. **Cost per Click (CPC)** - Monitor vs. estimates
4. **Cost per Conversion** - Target: $35-58
5. **Quality Score** - Target: 7+
6. **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)** - Target: 3:1

### Conversion Events to Track
1. Free trial signup
2. Paid plan signup
3. Digital footprint scan completion
4. Content generation (first piece)
5. Enterprise contact form submission
