**Tier 2: Secondary Markets (Month 2+)**

- **Netherlands** - High SaaS adoption, English-friendly
- **Sweden** - Tech-forward, high SaaS adoption
- **Ireland** - English-speaking, EU-based

**Tier 3: Expansion Markets (Month 3+)**
- **Singapore** - Asia-Pacific hub, English-speaking
- **United Arab Emirates** - Middle East hub, English-speaking
- **India** - Large market, English-speaking (lower CPC)
- **Brazil** - Large market (Portuguese ads needed)
- **Mexico** - Latin America hub (Spanish ads needed)

### Location Targeting Settings

**Campaign Type:** Location targeting (not location of interest)

**Exclude:**
- Countries with low B2B SaaS adoption
- Countries with language barriers (unless localized)
- Countries with payment processing issues

**Recommended Settings:**
- **People in or regularly in your targeted locations** (not "people searching for your targeted locations")
- **Exclude:** None initially, add exclusions based on performance data

### Location-Specific Considerations

**United States:**
- Highest CPC but highest conversion potential
- Target all states initially, optimize by state after 2 weeks
- Consider excluding low-performing states (e.g., rural areas with low B2B density)

**United Kingdom:**
- Similar CPC to US, strong B2B market
- GDPR compliance already handled (EU-based infrastructure)
- High conversion rates expected

**Canada:**
- Lower CPC than US, similar conversion rates
- English and French markets (consider French ads for Quebec)

**Australia:**
- Good B2B SaaS market, slightly lower CPC than US/UK
- Strong conversion rates

**EU Countries (Germany, France, Netherlands, etc.):**
- GDPR compliance required (AIBC already has this)
- Consider localized ads in local languages
- Lower CPC than US/UK typically
- Start with English ads, add localized versions based on performance
