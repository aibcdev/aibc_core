# How to change or cancel your subscription

You can change your subscription tier or cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.

## Changing Your Subscription

### Upgrading Your Plan

**To upgrade to a higher tier:**

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** section
2. Find **"Current Plan"** section
3. Click **"Upgrade Plan"** or **"Change Plan"**
4. Select the tier you want (Pro or Enterprise)
5. Review the features included
6. Click **"Upgrade"**
7. Complete payment if required
8. Your new plan is active immediately

**What happens when you upgrade:**
- New features are available immediately
- Higher limits take effect right away
- You may be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle
- Your billing cycle date remains the same

### Downgrading Your Plan

**To downgrade to a lower tier:**

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** section
2. Find **"Current Plan"** section
3. Click **"Change Plan"** or **"Downgrade"**
4. Select the lower tier you want
5. **Review warning message** about features you'll lose
6. Confirm you want to downgrade
7. Click **"Downgrade"**

**What happens when you downgrade:**
- Changes take effect at the end of your current billing period
- You keep access to current tier features until period ends
- You may receive a prorated refund (if applicable)
- Some features may be restricted immediately
- Content over new limits may be paused

**Important:** Review what features you'll lose before downgrading.

## Canceling Your Subscription

### How to Cancel

**To cancel your subscription:**

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** section
2. Find **"Subscription"** section
3. Click **"Cancel Subscription"** or **"Manage Subscription"**
4. You may be asked to confirm cancellation
5. Select reason for canceling (optional)
6. Click **"Confirm Cancellation"**

### What Happens When You Cancel

**Immediate:**
- Subscription is marked for cancellation
- No further charges will be made
- You keep access until end of billing period

**At end of billing period:**
- Access to paid features ends
- Account may revert to Free tier (if applicable)
- Data is retained for 30 days
- You can reactivate within 30 days

**After 30 days:**
- Data may be permanently deleted
- Cannot reactivate without starting over
- Export your data before this period ends

### Reactivating After Cancellation

**To reactivate within 30 days:**

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **"Reactivate Subscription"**
3. Select your plan
4. Complete payment
5. Access is restored immediately

**After 30 days:**
- Must sign up as new customer
- Previous data may not be available
- Start fresh with new account

## Billing Cycle Information

### When Changes Take Effect

**Upgrades:**
- Immediate activation
- Prorated charge for remainder of cycle

**Downgrades:**
- At end of current billing period
- Prorated refund may apply

**Cancellations:**
- At end of current billing period
- Access continues until period ends

### Prorated Billing

**How it works:**
- **Upgrades**: Pay difference for remaining days
- **Downgrades**: Receive refund for remaining days (if applicable)
- **Cancellations**: No refund for current period (unless specified)

**Example:**
- You're on day 15 of 30-day cycle
- You upgrade: Pay 50% of upgrade cost for remaining 15 days
- You downgrade: Receive 50% refund for remaining 15 days (if applicable)

## Refunds

### Refund Policy

**Standard policy:**
- No refunds for partial billing periods
- Refunds only in exceptional circumstances
- Contact support for refund requests

**When refunds may apply:**
- Service outages or issues
- Billing errors
- Special circumstances (contact support)

**How to request:**
- Email: support@aibcmedia.com
- Subject: "Refund Request"
- Include: Account details, reason, billing information

## Managing Payment Methods

### Updating Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Find **"Payment Method"** section
3. Click **"Update Payment Method"**
4. Enter new card details
5. Save changes

### Payment Issues

**If payment fails:**
- You'll receive email notification
- Account may be temporarily restricted
- Update payment method to restore access
- Contact support if issues persist

## Accessing Billing Portal

**Stripe Customer Portal:**
- Some plans use Stripe customer portal
- Access from Settings → Billing
- Manage subscription, payment methods, invoices
- Self-service subscription management
