# Manage Your Billing and Subscription Details

Manage your payment methods, view invoices, update billing information, and configure subscription settings from your account.

## Accessing Billing Settings

1. Go to **Settings** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"Billing"** section
3. You'll see all billing and subscription options

## Payment Methods

### Adding a Payment Method

**To add a new card:**
1. In **Billing** section, find **"Payment Methods"**
2. Click **"Add Payment Method"** or **"Update Payment Method"**
3. **Enter card details:**
   - Card number
   - Expiration date
   - CVV
   - Billing address
   - Cardholder name
4. Click **"Save"** or **"Add Card"**

**Supported payment methods:**
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Debit cards (where supported)

### Updating Payment Method

**To update existing card:**
1. Find current payment method
2. Click **"Edit"** or **"Update"**
3. Enter new card details
4. Click **"Save"**

**When to update:**
- Card expired
- Card replaced
- Changing primary payment method
- Updating billing address

### Setting Default Payment Method

**If you have multiple cards:**
1. Find payment methods list
2. Click **"Set as Default"** on preferred card
3. Default card is used for automatic payments

### Removing Payment Method

**To remove a card:**
1. Find payment method
2. Click **"Remove"** or **"Delete"**
3. Confirm removal
4. **Note:** Cannot remove if it's the only payment method

## Viewing Invoices

### Accessing Invoices

**To view invoices:**
1. In **Billing** section, find **"Invoices"** or **"Billing History"**
2. **See list of invoices:**
   - Invoice number
   - Date
   - Amount
   - Status (Paid, Pending, Failed)
   - Description

### Downloading Invoices

**To download:**
1. Find invoice in list
2. Click **"Download"** or **"View"**
3. Invoice opens in new tab or downloads as PDF
4. Save for your records

### Invoice Details

**Each invoice shows:**
- Invoice number
- Issue date
- Due date
- Amount charged
- Payment method used
- Itemized charges
- Tax/VAT (if applicable)
- Total amount

## Updating Billing Email

**To change billing email:**
1. In **Billing** section, find **"Billing Information"**
2. Find **"Billing Email"** field
3. Enter new email address
4. Click **"Save"**
5. **Verify email** (confirmation sent to new email)

**Why update:**
- Receive invoices at different email
- Separate billing from account email
- Update contact information

## Tax/VAT Information

### Adding Tax Information

**For tax-exempt organizations:**
1. In **Billing** section, find **"Tax Information"**
2. **Enter tax details:**
   - Tax ID number
   - Country/region
   - Tax-exempt status (if applicable)
3. Upload tax exemption certificate (if required)
4. Click **"Save"**

### Updating Tax Information

**To update:**
1. Find **"Tax Information"** section
2. Edit tax details
3. Upload new documents if needed
4. Click **"Save"**

**Tax/VAT applies:**
- Based on your location
- May vary by country/region
- Check local tax laws
- Contact support for tax questions

## Billing Address

### Updating Billing Address

**To update address:**
1. In **Billing** section, find **"Billing Address"**
2. **Enter new address:**
   - Street address
   - City
   - State/Province
   - ZIP/Postal code

## Payment History

### Viewing Payment History

**See all payments:**
1. In **Billing** section, find **"Payment History"**
2. **View:**
   - Payment date
   - Amount
   - Payment method
   - Status
   - Invoice link

### Payment Status

**Status types:**
- **Paid**: Successfully processed
- **Pending**: Processing
- **Failed**: Payment declined
- **Refunded**: Refund issued

## Managing Automatic Payments

### Enabling Auto-Pay

**Auto-pay is enabled by default:**
- Charges automatically on billing date
- Uses default payment method
- No action needed each month

### Disabling Auto-Pay

**To disable:**
1. Find **"Automatic Payments"** section
2. Toggle **"Auto-Pay"** off
3. **Warning:** Subscription may be cancelled if payment fails
4. You'll need to pay manually each month

## Billing Notifications

### Email Notifications

**Receive emails for:**
- Invoice issued
- Payment successful
- Payment failed
- Subscription changes
- Upcoming renewal

**To update notification preferences:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Configure billing email preferences
3. Save changes

## Payment Issues

### Failed Payments

**If payment fails:**
- **Email notification** sent immediately
- **Account status** may be affected
- **Access** may be restricted
- **Update payment method** to restore access

**To fix:**
1. Check payment method is valid
2. Update card if expired
3. Ensure sufficient funds
4. Contact bank if card is blocked
5. Update payment method in Settings

### Disputed Charges

**If you dispute a charge:**
- Contact support immediately
- Provide invoice details
- Explain the dispute
- We'll investigate and resolve

## Security
