# AIBC Pricing and Plans

AIBC offers three subscription tiers designed for different needs: Free, Pro, and Enterprise. Each tier includes different features, content generation limits, and credit allowances.

## Subscription Tiers

### Free Tier

**Monthly cost:** $0

**What's included:**
- **Content generation**: Up to 10 pieces per week
- **Brand Scan**: 1 Standard Scan per month
- **Workspaces**: 1 workspace
- **Seats**: 1 user
- **Integrations**: Basic social media connections
- **Content types**: Social posts, basic blog posts
- **Brand Kit**: Full access to all Brand Kit features
- **Analytics**: Basic analytics
- **Support**: Email support

**Limitations:**
- Limited content generation
- Standard scans only (no Deep Scan)
- Basic integrations
- No Production Room access (AI image/audio/video generation)
- Watermarks on some content (if applicable)

**Best for:**
- Individuals getting started
- Testing the platform
- Small personal brands
- Low-volume content needs

### Pro Tier

**Monthly cost:** $49/month

**What's included:**
- **Content generation**: Up to 50 pieces per week
- **Brand Scan**: 3 Standard Scans per month, 1 Deep Scan per month
- **Workspaces**: 1 workspace
- **Seats**: 1 user
- **Integrations**: All social media, analytics, email platforms
- **Content types**: All content types (social, blog, email, video scripts)
- **Brand Kit**: Full access with advanced features
- **Analytics**: Advanced analytics with competitive comparison
- **Production Room**: Image generation access
- **Credits**: 100 credits per month
- **Support**: Priority email support

**Additional features:**
- Competitor analysis
- Advanced content preferences
- Content templates
- Bulk operations
- Export capabilities

**Best for:**
- Small businesses
- Content creators
- Marketing teams (single user)
- Regular content publishing

### Enterprise Tier

**Monthly cost:** $149/month

**What's included:**
- **Content generation**: Unlimited
- **Brand Scan**: Unlimited Standard Scans, 5 Deep Scans per month
- **Workspaces**: 3 workspaces (multiple brands)
- **Seats**: 3 users
- **Integrations**: All integrations, including custom/API access
- **Content types**: All content types, including custom formats
- **Brand Kit**: Full access with white-label options
- **Analytics**: Full analytics suite with custom reports
- **Production Room**: Full access (images, audio, video)
- **Credits**: 500 credits per month
- **Support**: Priority support with dedicated account manager

**Additional features:**
- Team collaboration tools
- Advanced approval workflows
- Custom integrations
- API access
- White-label options
- Custom branding
- Advanced analytics
- Dedicated support

**Best for:**
- Agencies
- Large businesses
- Multiple brands
- High-volume content needs
- Teams requiring collaboration

## Add-Ons

### Extra Credits

**Available for:** Pro and Enterprise

**Pricing:**
- **Credit packs**: Purchase additional credits as needed
- **Pricing varies** by credit amount
- **Check current pricing** in Settings → Billing

**When to use:**
- Exceeding monthly credit allowance
- One-time high-volume needs
- Special campaigns or projects

### Additional Workspaces

**Available for:** Enterprise (included), can be added to Pro

**Pricing:**
- **Per workspace**: Additional monthly fee
- **Each workspace**: Separate brand/business
- **Includes**: Full feature access per workspace

**When to use:**
- Managing multiple brands
- Client accounts (agencies)
- Separate business lines

### Additional Seats

**Available for:** Enterprise (included), can be added to Pro

**Pricing:**
- **Per seat**: Additional monthly fee
- **Each seat**: Full user access
- **Team collaboration**: Enabled with multiple seats

**When to use:**
- Growing team
- Need collaboration features
- Multiple content creators

## Credit System

### What Are Credits?

Credits are used for:
- **AI image generation**: Production Room images
- **AI audio generation**: Production Room audio
- **AI video generation**: Production Room videos
- **Content regeneration**: Regenerating content
- **Deep Scans**: Premium scan type

### Credit Allowances

- **Free**: No credits (no Production Room access)
- **Pro**: 100 credits per month
- **Enterprise**: 500 credits per month

### Credit Costs

**Production Room:**
- **Image (Short)**: ~5 credits
- **Image (Mid)**: ~10 credits
- **Image (Long)**: ~20 credits
- **Audio (Short)**: ~15 credits
- **Audio (Mid)**: ~30 credits
- **Audio (Long)**: ~50 credits
- **Video (Short)**: ~25 credits
- **Video (Mid)**: ~50 credits
- **Video (Long)**: ~100 credits

**Other actions:**
- **Content regeneration**: ~1-2 credits per regeneration
- **Deep Scan**: Uses scan credits (separate from monthly credits)

### Managing Credits

**Check balance:**
- Dashboard shows credit balance
- Settings → Billing shows detailed usage
- Production Room shows costs before generating

**Purchase more:**
- Settings → Billing → Purchase Credits
- Select credit pack
- Complete payment
- Credits added immediately

## Choosing the Right Plan

### Start with Free

**If you:**
- Want to test the platform
- Have low content volume needs
- Are just getting started
- Want to explore features

**Upgrade when:**
- You need more content
- You want Production Room access
- You need more scans
- You're ready to scale

### Choose Pro

**If you:**
- Publish content regularly
- Need image generation
- Want advanced features
- Are a small business or creator

**Upgrade when:**
- You need multiple workspaces
- You need team collaboration
- You need unlimited content
- You need API access

### Choose Enterprise

**If you:**
- Manage multiple brands
- Have a team
- Need high volume
- Require advanced features
- Want white-label options

- **Tax/VAT**: Included where applicable

## Upgrading or Downgrading

### Upgrading

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **"Upgrade Plan"**
3. Select new tier
4. Complete payment
5. New features available immediately
6. Prorated billing (if applicable)

### Downgrading

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **"Change Plan"**
3. Select lower tier
4. **Warning**: Some features may be lost
5. Changes take effect at end of billing cycle
6. Prorated refund (if applicable)

## Free Trial

**Trial period:** 14 days

**What's included:**

- Cancel anytime during trial
- No charges if canceled before trial ends

**How to start:**
- Select Pro or Enterprise plan

## Non-Profits and Education

**Special pricing available:**
- **Eligibility**: Verified non-profits and educational institutions
- **Discount**: Up to 50% off
- **How to apply**: Contact support with verification
- **Verification required**: 501(c)(3) status or educational institution proof

## Cancellation

**How to cancel:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Access continues until end of billing period
5. No refunds for partial periods (unless specified)

**After cancellation:**
- Access continues until period ends
- Data is retained for 30 days
- Can reactivate within 30 days
- After 30 days, data may be deleted
