## Root Cause Analysis

### Content Ideas Generation Issues

**Problem Location:** `backend/src/services/scanService.ts` lines 987-1048

**Issues Identified:**

1. **Fallback Mechanism Override:**
   - Lines 1026-1036: If LLM generates less than 5 ideas, generic fallback ideas are added
   - Lines 1039-1047: If LLM generation fails, generic fallback ideas are used
   - **Problem:** Generic fallbacks are too generic and not tailored

2. **Prompt May Not Be Strong Enough:**
   - Lines 990-1013: The prompt includes brand DNA, themes, and posts
   - **Problem:** The prompt may not be emphasizing brand-specificity strongly enough
   - **Problem:** The LLM may be generating generic ideas despite the prompt

3. **No Validation of Brand Alignment:**
   - **Problem:** There's no validation to ensure content ideas align with brand DNA
   - **Problem:** Generic ideas can slip through even if LLM generates some tailored ones

### Competitor Intelligence: Working Well

**Why It Works:**
- ‚úÖ Deep niche analysis (lines 3525-3560) ensures accurate competitor matching
- ‚úÖ Multi-step approach (niche identification ‚Üí competitor matching)
- ‚úÖ Strong prompts emphasizing real competitor names
- ‚úÖ Industry-specific context is well-handled
