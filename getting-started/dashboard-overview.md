# Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your command center in AIBC. It provides an at-a-glance view of your content pipeline, strategic insights, brand DNA, and key metrics.

## What the Dashboard Shows

When you navigate to **Dashboard** (`/dashboard`), you'll see:

### Overview Metrics

**Content Pipeline Status:**
- **Draft**: Content being created
- **Needs Review**: Content awaiting approval
- **Scheduled**: Content set to publish
- **Published**: Live content
- **Total**: Overall content count

**Quick Stats:**
- Content generated this week
- Content scheduled for this week
- Content published this week
- Approval queue size

### Strategic Insights

**Key insights from your Brand Scan:**
- **Brand positioning**: Your market position
- **Content opportunities**: Areas to focus on
- **Competitive advantages**: What makes you unique
- **Market trends**: Industry insights
- **Recommendations**: Actionable next steps

**Insight cards show:**
- Insight title and description
- Priority level (High, Medium, Low)
- Related tags
- Action buttons

### Brand DNA Summary

**Your brand's core identity:**
- **Archetype**: Brand personality (e.g., "The Creator", "The Sage")
- **Voice Tone**: Primary communication tone
- **Core Pillars**: Main themes and values
- **Visual Identity**: Colors and style

**Extracted from:**
- Your Brand Scan results
- Your digital footprint analysis
- Your source materials

### Competitor Intelligence

**Top competitors and market position:**
- **Competitor list**: Tracked competitors
- **Threat levels**: High, Medium, Low
- **Market share**: Your position vs. competitors
- **Competitive insights**: How you compare

**Competitor cards show:**
- Competitor name and handle
- Threat level indicator
- Key metrics comparison
- Action items

### Content Pipeline Visualization

**Visual representation of content flow:**
- **Pipeline stages**: Draft → Review → Scheduled → Published
- **Content count**: Number in each stage
- **Bottlenecks**: Where content is stuck
- **Flow indicators**: Visual flow between stages

**Pipeline view helps:**
- Identify bottlenecks
- See content distribution
- Track workflow efficiency
- Plan capacity

### Analytics Quick View

**Performance at a glance:**
- **Engagement rate**: Overall engagement
- **Top platform**: Best performing platform
- **Content performance**: Recent top performers
- **Trend indicators**: Up/down arrows

**Quick metrics:**
- Impressions/reach
- Engagement counts
- Click-through rates
- Conversion metrics (if tracked)

### Credit Balance

**Your current credit usage:**
- **Total credits**: Monthly credit allowance
- **Used credits**: Credits consumed
- **Remaining credits**: Available balance
- **Usage percentage**: Visual indicator

**Credit display:**
- Shows tier limits
- Tracks usage
- Warns when low
- Links to upgrade (if needed)

### Notifications

**Recent activity and alerts:**
- **Scan complete**: Brand Scan finished
- **Content generated**: New content ready
- **Approvals needed**: Content waiting for review
- **Publishing status**: Success/failure alerts
- **System updates**: Platform notifications

**Notification types:**
- Success (green)
- Warning (yellow)
- Error (red)
- Info (blue)

### Tasks

**Action items and reminders:**
- **Review content**: Items needing approval
- **Update Brand Kit**: Optimization suggestions
- **Connect integrations**: Platform connections needed
- **Schedule content**: Content ready to schedule
- **Follow up**: Action items from insights

**Task management:**
- Mark as complete
- Set due dates
- Add notes
- Prioritize

## Interpreting Dashboard Metrics

### Content Pipeline Health

**Healthy pipeline:**
- Steady flow through stages
- No bottlenecks
- Balanced distribution
- Regular publishing

**Issues to watch:**
- Content stuck in review
- Too many drafts
- Nothing scheduled
- Publishing failures

### Strategic Insights Priority

**High priority:**
- Action immediately
- Significant impact
- Time-sensitive

**Medium priority:**
- Plan to address
- Moderate impact
- Can wait

**Low priority:**
- Nice to have
- Minor impact
- Optional

### Credit Balance Management

**Healthy usage:**
- Using credits regularly
- Not hitting limits
- Room for growth

**Warning signs:**
- Approaching limit
- Not using credits
- Need to upgrade
