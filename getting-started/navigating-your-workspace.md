# Navigating your Workspace

Your AIBC workspace is your command center for content creation, management, and publishing. This guide covers all 11 main pages and how to navigate between them.

## Workspace Overview

When you log into AIBC, you'll see the main workspace with:
- **Left sidebar navigation**: Access to all main pages
- **Top bar**: User profile, notifications, credits, settings
- **Main content area**: The current page you're viewing

## Main Navigation Pages

AIBC has 11 main pages accessible from the left sidebar:

1. **Dashboard** - Overview and metrics
2. **Content Hub** - Content ideas and generation
3. **Strategy** - Strategic insights and AI chat
4. **Production Room** - AI-generated media (images, audio, video)
5. **Calendar** - Publishing schedule and timeline
6. **Brand Assets** - Brand Kit configuration
7. **Integrations** - Platform connections
8. **Competitors** - Competitor tracking and analysis
9. **Analytics** - Performance metrics and insights
10. **Settings** - Account and preferences
11. **Inbox** - Content review and approvals

## 1. Dashboard (`/dashboard`)

**What it shows:**
- **Overview metrics**: Content pipeline status, tasks, upcoming deadlines
- **Strategic insights**: Key insights from your Brand Scan
- **Brand DNA summary**: Your brand's voice, tone, and pillars
- **Competitor intelligence**: Top competitors and market position
- **Content pipeline**: What's in draft, review, scheduled, published
- **Analytics quick view**: Performance at a glance
- **Credit balance**: Your current credit usage
- **Notifications**: Recent activity and alerts
- **Tasks**: Action items and reminders

**When to use it:**
- Starting your day to see what needs attention
- Checking overall status and metrics
- Viewing strategic insights
- Managing tasks and deadlines

**Key features:**
- Real-time updates
- Quick actions (approve, schedule, edit)
- Links to detailed pages
- Customizable widgets (coming soon)
