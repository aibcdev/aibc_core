How AIBC Thinks About Content

AIBC doesn't just generate generic content. It uses your Brand DNA - extracted from your Digital Footprint Scan - to create content that authentically matches how you communicate.

The Content Generation Process

1

Analyze Your Brand DNA

AIBC reviews your Brand DNA extracted from the scan: voice tone, vocabulary, themes, visual identity, and engagement patterns.

2

Apply Your Voice Settings

Uses your Brand Kit settings: tone preferences, vocabulary, do's and don'ts, content preferences, and topics to avoid.

3

Reference Source Materials

Learns from your uploaded source materials - your best blog posts, social posts, brand guidelines - to match your writing style.

4

Generate Content

Creates content that matches your voice, uses your vocabulary, follows your style, and addresses your topics - all while staying on-brand.

What Makes AIBC Content Different

Authentic Voice

Content sounds like you wrote it, not like generic AI content.

On-Brand Topics

Covers topics you actually talk about, avoids what you don't.

Visual Consistency

Matches your visual identity - colors, style, imagery preferences.

Performance Optimized

Learns from your best-performing content to replicate success.

The More You Teach, The Better It Gets

Every time you edit content, use the "Update Agent" feature, or add source materials, AIBC learns and improves. Your content quality increases over time.

Audio Generation in Production Room

AIBC's Production Room lets you generate audio content - from podcast intros and outros to background music - all matching your brand's style and voice.

Available Audio Types

Podcast Intros & Outros

Generate branded podcast introductions and conclusions that match your show's tone and style.

Credit cost: ~15-50 credits depending on length

Background Music

Create background music tracks that match your brand's energy and style for videos and presentations.

Credit cost: ~15-50 credits depending on length

Sound Effects

Generate custom sound effects and audio elements for your content.

Credit cost: ~10-30 credits depending on complexity

How to Generate Audio

  1. Navigate to Production Room from your dashboard
  2. Select "Audio" as output type
  3. Choose duration: Short (~15-30s), Mid (~30-60s), or Long (60s+)
  4. Select style: Energetic, Professional, Calm, etc.
  5. Add description of what you want (e.g., "Upbeat podcast intro for tech show")
  6. Check credit cost and your balance
  7. Click "Generate"
  8. Audio is added to production queue and will be ready shortly

Audio Generation Requirements

Audio generation is available on Enterprise tier only. Pro tier has access to image generation, but audio and video require Enterprise subscription.

Check your credit balance before generating - audio generation uses credits from your monthly allowance.

Video Scaling and Generation

Scale your video content production with AIBC. Generate videos from shorts and reels to long-form content, all matching your brand's visual identity and style.

Video Types You Can Generate

Short-Form Videos

15-60 second videos for Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts

Credit cost: ~25 credits

Mid-Length Videos

1-5 minute videos for YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook

Credit cost: ~50 credits

Long-Form Videos

5+ minute videos for YouTube, webinars, presentations

Credit cost: ~100 credits

Video Templates

Pre-designed video templates matching your brand style

Credit cost: Varies by template

Video Generation Process

When generating videos, AIBC:

  • Uses your Brand DNA to match visual style (colors, imagery, tone)
  • Applies your brand's voice and messaging
  • Matches aspect ratios for target platforms (9:16 for Reels, 16:9 for YouTube, etc.)
  • Incorporates your brand elements (logos, colors, fonts) when available
  • Generates content that aligns with your topics and themes

Scaling Video Production

AIBC helps you scale video production by:

Batch generation: Create multiple videos at once for campaigns or series

Consistent branding: All videos match your brand automatically

Platform optimization: Videos automatically formatted for each platform

Content planning: Plan and schedule video content alongside other content types

Video Generation Requirements

Video generation requires Enterprise tier subscription. This is the most resource-intensive feature and requires significant computational resources.
