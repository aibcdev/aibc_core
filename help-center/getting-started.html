Setting Up Your Digital Footprint Scan

The Digital Footprint Scan is the foundation of AIBC. It analyzes your brand's existing content across platforms to understand how you communicate, what you stand for, and how you engage with your audience.

What AIBC Scans

AIBC scans only your brand's own output - content you've published yourself. We do NOT collect:

  • Mentions or discussions about your brand
  • Third-party content about your brand
  • User-generated content mentioning your brand
  • Comments or reactions (unless from your own account)

Platforms Scanned

AIBC scans these platforms:

Twitter/X

LinkedIn

Instagram

YouTube

How to Run a Scan

  1. Navigate to the Scan page from your dashboard
  2. Enter your website URL or social media username
  3. Select which platforms to scan (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube)
  4. Choose scan type:
    • Standard Scan (Free/Pro): LLM-powered web scanning, ~$0.10-0.50 per scan
    • Deep Scan (Enterprise): Official API access with 10-year history, ~$7.00 per scan
  5. Click "Scan Digital Footprint"
  6. Wait for scan to complete (typically under 24 hours for standard scans)

Pro Tip

The more content you have published, the better AIBC understands your brand. If you're just starting out, consider running a Deep Scan (Enterprise tier) to get more comprehensive data.

What Happens During the Scan

During the scan, AIBC:

  • Extracts all content you've published on selected platforms
  • Analyzes your writing style, tone, and vocabulary
  • Identifies your visual identity (colors, imagery, style)
  • Understands your engagement patterns and content preferences
  • Extracts your Brand DNA (archetype, voice, core pillars)
  • Identifies competitors (if enabled)

After the Scan Completes

Once your scan is complete, you'll have:

  • Brand DNA stored in your Brand Kit
  • Strategic insights about your brand position
  • Content ideas generated based on your voice
  • Competitor intelligence (if enabled)

You can now start generating content that authentically matches your brand voice!
