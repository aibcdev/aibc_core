Workspace Roles and Permissions

AIBC offers four workspace roles to control what team members can do. Roles are available on Enterprise tier (3 seats included).

Role Types

Admin

Full access to everything in the workspace.

  • All content creation and editing
  • Brand Kit configuration
  • Platform integrations
  • Workspace settings and billing
  • Invite and manage team members

Editor

Can create, edit, and publish content.

  • Create and edit content
  • Schedule and publish content
  • View Brand Kit (read-only)
  • Cannot modify Brand Kit or settings
  • Cannot manage integrations or billing

Reviewer

Can review and approve content before publishing.

  • View all content
  • Approve or reject content
  • Add comments and feedback
  • Cannot create or edit content
  • Cannot modify Brand Kit or settings

Guest

Limited access for external reviewers or clients.

  • View shared content only
  • Add comments on shared content
  • Cannot access workspace directly
  • Access via share links only

Team Features Availability

Team collaboration features (multiple seats, roles, permissions) are available on Enterprise tier only. Free and Pro tiers include 1 seat per workspace.

Inviting Team Members

Invite team members to collaborate on content creation, review, and publishing. Team features are available on Enterprise tier.

How to Invite Team Members

  1. Go to Settings â†’ Team section
  2. Click "Invite Team Member" button
  3. Enter team member's email address
  4. Select their role:
    • Admin - Full access
    • Editor - Create and edit content
    • Reviewer - Review and approve content
  5. Click "Send Invitation"
  6. Team member receives email invitation
  7. They accept invitation and join workspace

Managing Team Members

As an Admin, you can:

  • Change team member roles
  • Remove team members from workspace
  • Resend invitations
  • View team member activity
