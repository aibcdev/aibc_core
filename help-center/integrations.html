How to Connect a Platform

  1. Navigate to Integrations page from your dashboard
  2. Find the platform you want to connect (e.g., Instagram, LinkedIn)
  3. Click "Connect" button
  4. Enter your account handle or username (e.g., @yourbrand)
  5. Click "Verify" - AIBC will verify the account exists
  6. Authorize AIBC access (you'll be redirected to the platform for OAuth)
  7. Grant necessary permissions (publishing, reading content, etc.)
  8. Connection is established - you'll see "Connected" status

Security & Permissions

AIBC only requests the minimum permissions needed to publish content. We never post without your explicit approval (unless auto-publish is enabled in your settings).

You can revoke access at any time from the Integrations page or from the platform's app settings.

Medium

Medium.com publications

Publish articles to your Medium publication or personal profile directly from AIBC.

Requires: Medium API access, publication permissions

How CMS Integration Works

When you connect a CMS:

  • AIBC can create blog posts and articles in your CMS
  • Content is formatted according to your CMS requirements
  • You can publish immediately or save as drafts
  • Images and media are uploaded to your CMS media library
  • SEO metadata is included automatically
