Troubleshooting

Solutions to common issues with scanning, rendering, or posting content.

Brand Scan Not Completing

Your Digital Footprint Scan is taking too long or failing to complete.

Check Scan Status

Go to Dashboard and check the scan progress. Standard scans typically take 2-12 hours. Deep scans can take 12-24 hours.

Verify Input

Ensure your website URL or username is correct and the account/profile is public and accessible.

Check Platform Access

Some platforms may have rate limits or require authentication. Try scanning fewer platforms at once.

Contact Support

If scan exceeds 48 hours, contact support with your scan ID for assistance.

Content Rendering Issues

Generated content doesn't look right, images aren't loading, or formatting is broken.

Clear Browser Cache

Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R) or clear browser cache to reload assets.

Check Image Formats

Ensure images are in supported formats (JPG, PNG, WebP) and under size limits for your platform.

Regenerate Content

If content looks wrong, try regenerating with a more specific brief or update your Brand Kit settings.

Posts Not Publishing

Scheduled or manual posts are failing to publish to connected platforms.

Verify Platform Connection

Go to Integrations and check that the platform shows "Connected" status. Reconnect if needed.

Check Platform Permissions

Ensure AIBC has publishing permissions. You may need to re-authorize the connection.

Review Content Requirements

Check that content meets platform requirements (character limits, image sizes, format requirements).

Check Auto-Publish Settings

Verify auto-publish is enabled in Settings → Content Preferences if you want automatic publishing.

Retry Failed Posts

Go to Content Hub, filter by "Failed" status, and retry publishing. Check error messages for details.

Content Quality Issues

Generated content feels generic, doesn't match your voice, or is off-topic.

Add Source Materials

Go to Brand Assets → Materials and add 5-10 examples of your best content. The agent learns primarily from examples.

Refine Voice Settings

Update Brand Assets → Voice tab with more specific tone, vocabulary, and do's/don'ts.

Set Topics to Avoid

In Brand Assets → Preferences, add topics and words to avoid to keep content on-brand.

Use Update Agent Feature

After editing content, use "Update Agent" so AIBC learns from your improvements.

Run a New Brand Scan

If your brand has evolved, run a new Brand Scan to update your Brand DNA with recent content.

Credit Balance Issues

Running out of credits, credits not resetting, or unclear about credit costs.

Check Credit Balance

View your balance in Dashboard or Settings → Billing. Credits reset monthly on your billing date.

Understand Credit Costs

Production Room shows credit costs before generating. Image: 5-20 credits, Audio: 15-50 credits, Video: 25-100 credits.

Purchase Additional Credits

Go to Settings → Billing → Purchase Credits to buy credit packs if you need more.

Upgrade Your Plan

Pro tier includes 100 credits/month, Enterprise includes 500 credits/month. Upgrade for more credits.
