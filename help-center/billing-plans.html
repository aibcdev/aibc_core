Understanding the Credit System

AIBC uses a credit system for Production Room features - AI-generated images, audio, and video. Regular content generation, Brand Scans, and most features don't use credits.

What Uses Credits

Image Generation

Generate AI images for social media, banners, graphics

5-20 credits

per image

Cost varies by complexity: Short/Simple (~5), Mid/Moderate (~10), Long/Complex (~20)

Audio Generation

Create podcast intros, outros, background music

15-50 credits

per audio

Enterprise tier only. Cost varies by length: Short (~15), Mid (~30), Long (~50)

Video Generation

Generate videos from shorts to long-form content

25-100 credits

per video

Enterprise tier only. Cost varies by length: Short (~25), Mid (~50), Long (~100)

What Doesn't Use Credits

  • Regular content generation (text posts, blogs, emails)
  • Standard Brand Scans
  • Content editing and regeneration (text content)
  • Platform integrations and publishing
  • Analytics and reporting

Managing Your Credits

Check your credit balance in the Dashboard or Production Room. Credits reset monthly on your billing date. You can purchase additional credit packs if you need more.

Pro tip: Use simpler styles when possible to conserve credits for important projects.

Choosing the Right Tier

Free Tier - Getting Started

Perfect for testing AIBC, understanding how Brand Scans work, and generating a small amount of content to see if AIBC fits your needs.

Best for: Individuals, personal brands, testing the platform

Pro Tier - Content Creators

Ideal for small businesses, content creators, and marketing teams who publish content regularly and need image generation capabilities.

Best for: Small businesses, content creators, regular publishers, image generation needs

Enterprise Tier - Agencies & Scale

Built for agencies managing multiple brands, high-volume content needs, and teams requiring audio/video generation and collaboration features.

Best for: Agencies, large businesses, multiple brands, high-volume content, audio/video needs
