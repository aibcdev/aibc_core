# Adding Team Members to Netlify

## ‚úÖ Yes! You Can Add Team Members

Netlify allows you to invite team members to your account/team. They can collaborate on sites, manage deployments, and access settings.

---

## How to Add Team Members

### Step 1: Access Team Settings

1. **Go to Netlify Dashboard:**
   - Visit: https://app.netlify.com
   - Click your **profile icon** (top right)
   - Select **"Team settings"** or **"Team overview"**

   OR

   - Go directly to: https://app.netlify.com/teams/YOUR_TEAM_NAME/members

### Step 2: Invite Team Members

1. **Click "Invite members"** button
2. **Enter email addresses:**
   - Add one or multiple email addresses
   - Each person will receive an invitation email
3. **Choose role** (if applicable):
   - **Owner** - Full access to everything
   - **Member** - Can manage sites and deployments
   - **Guest** - Limited access (Pro/Business plans)

4. **Click "Send invitations"**

---

## Team Member Roles

### Owner
- Full access to all sites
- Can manage team settings
- Can add/remove members
- Can change billing

### Member
- Can manage sites and deployments
- Can view team sites
- Cannot change team settings or billing
- Cannot remove other members

### Guest (Pro/Business only)
- Limited to specific sites
- Can only access sites they're invited to
- Cannot see other team sites

## Managing Team Members

### View All Members:
- Go to **Team settings** ‚Üí **Members**
- See all invited and active members
- View their roles and status

### Remove Members:
1. Go to **Team settings** ‚Üí **Members**
2. Find the member
3. Click **"Remove"** or **"..."** menu
4. Confirm removal

### Change Roles:
1. Go to **Team settings** ‚Üí **Members**
2. Click on member
3. Change their role
4. Save changes

---

## Site-Specific Access

### Invite to Specific Site (Pro/Business):

1. **Go to Site Settings:**
   - Select a site
   - Go to **"Access control"** or **"Team"**

2. **Add Collaborator:**
   - Click **"Add collaborator"**
   - Enter email
   - Choose permissions for that site

3. **Permissions:**
   - **Admin** - Full site access
   - **Editor** - Can deploy and manage
   - **Viewer** - Read-only access

---

## Invitation Process

1. **You send invitation:**
   - Enter email in team settings
   - Click "Send invitations"

2. **Team member receives email:**
   - Gets invitation email from Netlify
   - Clicks "Accept invitation"
   - Creates Netlify account (if needed) or logs in

3. **They're added:**
   - Can now see team sites
   - Can collaborate based on their role

---

## Best Practices

### For Small Teams:
- Add everyone as **Members**
- Everyone can manage all sites
- Simple and effective

### For Larger Teams:
- Use **Guest** roles (Pro/Business)
- Assign site-specific access
- Limit who can change settings

### Security:
- Only invite trusted team members
- Review member list regularly
- Remove access when team members leave

---

## Troubleshooting

### "Invitation not received"
- Check spam folder
- Verify email address is correct
- Resend invitation

### "Can't add member"
- Check your plan limits
- Free tier has member limits
- Upgrade to Pro for more features

### "Member can't see sites"
- Check their role/permissions
- Verify they accepted invitation
- Check site-specific access settings
