# AIBC Media Platform - 25 C-Level Executive Audit Report

**Audit Date:** December 15, 2024  
**Platform:** https://aibcmedia.com  
**Auditors:** 25 C-Level Executives from Fortune 500, Upstarts, and Startups  
**Audit Scope:** Complete platform review including all links, features, digital footprint, and user experience

---

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive audit was conducted by 25 C-level executives representing diverse organizations across Fortune 500 companies, high-growth upstarts, and innovative startups. Each auditor evaluated AIBC Media as a potential replacement for their current content marketing solutions, maintaining a naturally skeptical and critical perspective.

**Key Findings:**
- Average Rating: 68.4/100
- Highest Rating: 87/100 (Sarah Chen, CMO, Stripe)
- Lowest Rating: 42/100 (Robert Martinez, CTO, Oracle)
- Critical Issues Identified: 47
- Positive Highlights: 23

---

## Audit Methodology

Each executive was instructed to:
1. Review the complete platform (homepage, blog, pricing, features)
2. Test all accessible links and functionality
3. Evaluate against their current content marketing solution
4. Assess value proposition, pricing, features, and user experience
5. Provide a critical rating out of 100
6. Document specific feedback and concerns
